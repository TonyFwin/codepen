Kanye West Timeline

  • June 8, 1977: Kanye is born in Douglasville, Ga.
  • 1980: Kanye moves to Chicago following his parents' divorce.
  • 1994: Graduates Polaris High SChool.
  • 1996: Kanye delivers a freestyle at Fat Beats storefront in Greenich Village.
  • 1998: Kanye sells his first beat to a local rapper, his next to Jermaine Dupri.
  • 2000: He begins to produce for Jay-Z and Roc-A-Fella records artists.
  • 2002: Kanye is injured in a near-fatal car crash after falling asleep at the wheel.
  • 2004: The College Dropout is released, selling 441,000 copies in its first week.
  • 2005: His sophomore album, Late Registration is released. Selling 860,000 in its first week.
  • 2007: Graduation, his third studio album, is released. Selling 957,000 units in its first week.
  • 2007: Later that year, Kanye's mother, Donda, dies due to complications after surgery.
  • 2008: 808s & Heartbreak is released. His fourth studio album.
  • 2009: Kanye speaks out against injustice at the grammys during Taylor Swifts acceptance speech.
  • 2010: Kanye releases his fifth studio album, My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy.
  • 2011: Jay Z and Kanye West release a joint album, Watch The Throne.
  • 2013: Kanye releases Yeezus. Later that year his first child, North, is born.
  • 2014: Kanye and Kim Kardashian get married.
  • 2015: Kanye debuts his Addidas line Yeezy Season. Later that year his son, Saint is born.
  • 2016: The Life of Pablo, his seventh studio album, is released.
  • 2016 cont: Taylor Swift revealed to be a manipulative snake. Proven to be a liar by Kim Kardashian West.
  • 2017: Kanye continues to be an example for all people around the world.
